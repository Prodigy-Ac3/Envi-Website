Events

The Environmental Club is involved in projects that promote environmental awareness and encourage responsible practices within the school community. These projects are designed to educate students on environmental issues while providing opportunities for active participation.

Environmental Awareness Day

This event focuses on increasing awareness of environmental issues within the school community. It provides an opportunity for students to learn about environmental responsibility and the importance of caring for the environment through shared activities and discussion.
