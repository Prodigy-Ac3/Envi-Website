Our Projects

The Environmental Club is involved in projects that promote environmental awareness and encourage responsible practices within the school community. These projects are designed to educate students on environmental issues while providing opportunities for active participation.

Environmental Awareness Campaign

This project focuses on raising awareness about environmental issues that affect the school community. Through participation and discussion, members are encouraged to develop a better understanding of environmental responsibility and the importance of sustainable practice.

School Clean-Up Initiative

This project encourages active involvement and initiatives that support environmental care. It provides students with opportunity to contribute positively to the school environment while learning how individual actions can have a meaningful impact.

Recycling And Waste Reduction Enterprise

This project aims to strengthen student engagement and environmental activities by promoting collaboration and participation. Members work together to support environmental awareness and promote positive environmental behavior within the school community.
