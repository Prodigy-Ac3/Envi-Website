About The Environmental Club

The Environmental Club is a student leader group dedicated to promoting environmental awareness and responsible practices within the school community. The club works to educate members on environmental issues and encourage participation in activities that support sustainability and environmental care.

Through its project and events, the Environmental Club provides students with opportunities to become actively involved in environmental initiatives. This website was created to share information about the club's purpose, activities and ongoing efforts, helping to strengthen communication and engagement within school community
